Minhyan Changelog   Advanced
8/29/04
Removed 'j' /zh/ from phonology, as it didn't occur in lexicon, and respelled /sh/ as 'j' instead of 'jj'.  Thanks to Roger Mills of Kash fame for pointing out that /zh/ wasn't used.
9/12/04
Uploaded Minhyan Translation Tutorial.
9/13/04
Thanks to Fen Yik (creator of Lûá) for pointing out the vestiges of /zh/ left on the pronunciation page.  Those are now removed. He also pointed out that the description of the accent was unclear; I've expanded that.  I also took the occasion to fix a few misspellings in the lexicon (-uu instead of -uw).  I also translated the Forest People's Rhyme.
9/30/04
Uploaded Mattias Persson's Minhyan scripts.
