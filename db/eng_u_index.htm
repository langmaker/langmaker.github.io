Letter U Neologisms   Advanced

uber - Ultra-, super-, over-, more than, to an extreme degree. Note: Used as a prefix or an independent word.

uberdexterwise - To the right and up a bit.

uglossia - The art of inventing languages.

ultrasupercoolerama - Expression of enthusiasm or agreement, esp. by someone who is unaware of how uncool the word actually sounds.

umbrescence - Ability to draw in light rather than put it out, casting well-lit areas into shadow.

undermind - The subconscious or the subconscious mind; the (often) unseen elements of the mind.

understandablew - Sucky but understandable.

undiscovery - Retraction of a claim to have discovered something

unibrow - A continuous eyebrow stretching over both eyes and the nose, rather than being two separate eyebrows. It's easy to look around and think you are the only one suffering from the unibrow problem. - Sharp Grooming

uniminy - To melt, unite, or assimilate two or more things or people into one, like in "two sayajins were uniminied into one"

uniquify - To make unique; to make one-of-a-kind.

unobtainium - A notional element or material whose non-existence accounts for the impracticality of accomplishing some engineering task. Example: "Sure, the brakes will work just fine at 300mph, if you make the rotors out of unobtainium."

unsackable - Unable to be sacked, having a job for life no matter what mistakes are made or what incompetence is demonstrated.

unter- - Below grade, second fiddle. "He had a car that was so unter-cool that even he had to sell it the minute he bought it!"

upside up - In such a way that the upper and the lower parts are no longer reversed in position.

upsize - To add to a company's personnel.

urchfont - Sudden stab of hypocrisy which goes through the mind when taking vows as a godparent. - Douglas Adams & John Lloyd

urple - The ugliest color ever (even worse than this), usually considered to be a ghastly mixture of pink and purple.

utopilang - A language meant to unite the world and make it peaceful and prosperous (e.g., Esperanto).

uxory - 1. Passionate attachment to a spouse. 2. Perfect blend of love and lust.
