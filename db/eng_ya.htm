ya (interj.) - Neologism Profile   Advanced
Wordya
Syllabificationya
Part of Speechinterj.
Other Formsyeah, yah, ja, jah, ah
Etymology[From old Germanic use of ja; the Hawaiian version is a contraction of the Hawaiian word `e`a. Generally considered to be coined by surfers of Hawaiian descent.] *This usage is also very common amongst Minnesotans and North Dakotans.
DefinitionUsed to ascertain or reinforce a listener's interest or agreement. Examples: "Makes you pine for the fjord, ya?" "That's a big wave, ah?"
