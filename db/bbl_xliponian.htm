Xliponian (The language of the Kingdom of Xliponia (Hoimtat Xliponia)) - Babel Text Profile   Advanced

LanguageXliponian
Language SubtitleThe language of the Kingdom of Xliponia (Hoimtat Xliponia)
Year1888
TranslatorRonald Kyrmse
IntroductionXliponian (the letter x is pronounced like a sh) is an Indo-European language spoken almost exclusively in the Kingdom of Xliponia. It is derived from the vulgar Latin of the Roman imperial conquerors who occupied the region in the early centuries of the Common Era. The main feature that distinguishes Xliponian from other Romance languages is the soundshift suffered very early by some consonantal sounds.
Verse 1In çer ert on serm ac on mot lincui.
Verse 2Fuit ha salirinn de orient ac inbenenn hamp in çer Xinar ac apiçarann inte.
Verse 3Ac tiqit om ad om, benit, vahem laçri ac hocuim tem in icen ac apuirunn laçri hom saqi ac peçuim hom himenn.
Verse 4Ac tiqirinn, benit, vahem nos hibiçait ac çur hui hulmin fertincat ad hail ac heliprem noim nosser ot non sermes tibitut in çeri çoti.
Verse 5Auçem tessennet Tomen ot biter hibiçait ac çur ha aiçficarann vili Adam.
Verse 6Ac tiqit, eh foxlo est on ac serm tenen est on, ac ete hoifrinn vaher, ac non tesistris a çot ha hocitas vaher.
Verse 7Eh benit teqindam ac hofunnam ipi serm tenen ot non autis om lincu a om.
Verse 8Aciç tibiti tem Tomen eq loh ilo in çeri çoti ac hesabann aiçficar hibiçait.
Verse 9Ac ihirc noim ei est bohut Babel hui ipi honvusiti Tomen serm a çer çot ac atte Tomen sfirsi tem sur vaih a çer çot.
