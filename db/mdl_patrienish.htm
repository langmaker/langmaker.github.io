Patrienish - Conlang Profile   Advanced
Language NamePatrienish
Language AuthorMike Brooker
Year Began1975
SitePatrienish
Broken LinkNo
Site LanguageEnglish
Language Typeinternational auxiliary language
UniquenessAlong with English and Sanskrit, Patrienish is the official language of the Inner Realm of Patria. Based on Latin/Romance family roots, Patrienish also contains some elements of Sanskrit grammar, such as a dual number, as well as a few Anglo-Saxon or Germanic elements. A "classical" version of Patrienish - with many Sanskrit borrowings and no definite/indefinite articles - has become the official standard version of Patrienish that is now taught in schools in Patria.
Language SourcesSanskrit, English, Latin, Afrikaans, Quenya
Design PrinciplesDual number, as well as singular and plural (as in Sanskrit). Inflected for 6 cases: nominative, genitive, dative, accusative, instrumental, locative.
Interest Of OthersI have received a few inquiries, but for more than 25 years Patrienish has been a one-man project.
Conculture Noun PhraseAntarbhumi Ramrajya, the Inner Realm of Patria, a micronation dating back to 1972
QuotationPeríful delivrán ísí santanán, fecamortán ísí malefítorán, remontán rítambarulítor, ín omnís epoquís ímí nasquïtí.
TranslationTo deliver the holy men, to destroy the evil-doers and to restore righteousness (dharma), I take birth in every age. - Bhagavadgita
