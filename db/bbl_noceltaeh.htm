Noceltaeh (Noceltaeh gósam) - Babel Text Profile   Advanced
SiteNoceltaeh
LanguageNoceltaeh
Language SubtitleNoceltaeh gósam
Year2003
TranslatorAllen Squires
IntroductionThis language is a combination of many rules of different languages and of course some of my own ideas.
Verse 1Eg challe aon gósam ardiv eg aon abayradh iye.
Verse 2Eg nelwmiv ethis; mhalomen vásáas myniv Sinaraos.
Verse 3Eg mhílaledlen, gwnella, bánwmella cwlwti eg caserella sen anno. Eg challen cwlwti aten deygod eg cetáwl vúdeomod.
Verse 4Mhílalen, gwnella, beedella bayl eg cedywl pura, déladellen gieldi enos nChla; bánwmella aym pura, go resaneflella myniv annuh.
Verse 5Eg chólape Antan nadsod a natónig ant bhayl eg ant chedywl, slen bheeden clani mylmah.
Verse 6Eg mhílale Antan, galesete, calle aon nílol, eg calle aon gósam aten; eg d?orynen she natwnig: eg callelle nyl sarycach enis, go velyfellen a natwnig.
Verse 7Gwna, nadsod, eg barófella gósam enos, ge vályneflelledlen.
Verse 8Ge dresane Antan sen ardiv annuh eg shocapen a beedig ant bhayl.
Verse 9Adsos calle aym Babel baylos; devrig bharófe Antan ant ghósami ayrd; adsos dresan Antan sen ardiv annuh.
