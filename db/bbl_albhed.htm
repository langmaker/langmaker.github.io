IntroductionAl Bhed is a language from Final Fantasy.
Verse 1Yht ymm dra ayndr ryt uha myhkiyka yht uha duhkia. /AE-ha-te aemm de-raah ahaen-de-ra raae-te oo-haah mae-ha-kukeeae-kukah ae-ha-te oo-haah deoo-ha-kukeeah/
Verse 2Yht ed lysa ypuid dryd eh draen fyhtanehk vnus dra aycd, drao lysa du y cdnadlr uv vmyd luihdno eh dra myht uv Crehyn, yht drana drao syta draen mejehk-bmyla. /AE-ha-te eay-de luae-seeah ae-peooee-de de-raae-de eay-ha de-raaheayn feae-ha-teahneay-ha-kuk funoo-see de-raah ahae-ku-de, de-raahoh luae-seeah deoo ae ku-denah-de-lu-ra oo-fu fumae-de luooee-ha-denoh eay-ha de-raah mae-ha-te oo-fu KU-raeay-haaen, ae-ha-te de-raahnah de-raahoh seeae-teah de-raaheayn meay-jaeeay-ha-kuk-baemae-luah/
Verse 3Yht drao cyet uha du yhudran, Lusa, mad ic syga pnelgc, pinhehk dras famm. Yht drao ryt pnelgc vun cduha, biddehk dras dukadran fedr cdelgo ayndr. /AE-ha-te de-raahoh kuaeeay-te oo-haah deoo ae-haoo-de-raahn, LUoo-seeah, mah-de ee-ku seeae-geah peneay-lu-ge-ku, peeen-haeay-ha-kuk de-raah-see feahmm. AE-ha-te de-raahoh raae-te peneay-lu-ge-ku fuoon ku-deoo-haah, baeee-de-deeay-ha-kuk de-raah-see deoo-kukah-de-raahn feeay-de-ra ku-deeay-lu-geoh ahaen-de-ra/
Verse 4Yht drao cyet, Lusa, mad ic syga y dufh, yht y dufan fruca dub femm ku ib yc rekr yc rayjah; yht mad ic syga y knayd hysa vun uincamjac, cu dryd fa syo hud pa fyhtananc ujan dra vyla uv dra ayndr. /AE-ha-te de-raahoh kuaeeay-te, LUoo-seeah, mah-de ee-ku seeae-geah ae deoo-fe-ha, ae-ha-te ae deoo-feahn fe-raoo-kuah deoo-bae feeaymm kukoo ee-bae ae-ku raeay-kuk-ra ae-ku raahae-jaeah-ha; ae-ha-te mah-de ee-ku seeae-geah ae kuknahae-de haae-seeah fuoon ooeen-kuahm-jaeah-ku, kuoo de-raae-de feah seeaeoh haoo-de peah feae-ha-teahnahn-ku oo-jaeahn de-raah fuae-luah oo-fu de-raah ahaen-de-ra/
Verse 5Yht dra Munt lysa tufh du caa dra dufh yht dra dufan frelr dra lremtnah uv sah fana piemtehk. /AE-ha-te de-raah Moon-te luae-seeah teoo-fe-ha deoo kuahah de-raah deoo-fe-ha ae-ha-te de-raah deoo-feahn fe-raeay-lu-ra de-raah lu-raeaym-tenah-ha oo-fu seeah-ha feahnah peeeeaym-teeay-ha-kuk/
Verse 6Yht dra Munt cyet, Caa, drao yna ymm uha baubma yht ryja ymm uha myhkiyka; yht drec ec uhmo dra cdynd uv fryd drao syo tu; yht huf ed femm hud pa buccepma du gaab dras vnus yho binbuca uv draenc. /AE-ha-te de-raah Moon-te kuaeeay-te, KUahah, de-raahoh aenah aemm oo-haah baeahoo-baemah ae-ha-te raae-jaeah aemm oo-haah mae-ha-kukeeae-kukah; ae-ha-te de-raeay-ku eay-ku oo-hamoh de-raah ku-deaen-de oo-fu fe-raae-de de-raahoh seeaeoh teoo; ae-ha-te haoo-fe eay-de feeaymm haoo-de peah baeoo-ku-kueay-pemah deoo geahah-bae de-raah-see funoo-see ae-haoh baeeen-baeoo-kuah oo-fu de-raaheayn-ku/
Verse 7Lusa, mad ic ku tufh yht dyga yfyo dra cahca uv draen myhkiyka, cu dryd drao femm hud pa ypma du syga drascamjac lmayn du uha yhudran. /LUoo-seeah, mah-de ee-ku kukoo teoo-fe-ha ae-ha-te deae-geah ae-feaeoh de-raah kuah-ha-kuah oo-fu de-raaheayn mae-ha-kukeeae-kukah, kuoo de-raae-de de-raahoh feeaymm haoo-de peah ae-pemah deoo seeae-geah de-raah-see-kuahm-jaeah-ku lumahaen deoo oo-haah ae-haoo-de-raahn/
Verse 8Cu dra Munt Kut cahd dras yfyo ehdu ajano bynd uv dra ayndr; yht drao kyja ib piemtehk draen dufh. /KUoo de-raah Moon-te KUKoo-te kuah-ha-de de-raah-see ae-feaeoh eay-ha-deoo ah-jaeahnoh baeaen-de oo-fu de-raah ahaen-de-ra; ae-ha-te de-raahoh kukae-jaeah ee-bae peeeeaym-teeay-ha-kuk de-raaheayn deoo-fe-ha/
Verse 9Cu ed fyc hysat Pypam, palyica drana dra Munt duug yfyo dra cahca uv ymm myhkiykac; yht vnus drana dra Munt cahd dras yfyo ujan ymm dra vyla uv dra ayndr. /KUoo eay-de feae-ku haae-seeah-te PEae-peahm, peah-luaeee-kuah de-raahnah de-raah Moon-te deoooo-ge ae-feaeoh de-raah kuah-ha-kuah oo-fu aemm mae-ha-kukeeae-kukah-ku; ae-ha-te funoo-see de-raahnah de-raah Moon-te kuah-ha-de de-raah-see ae-feaeoh oo-jaeahn aemm de-raah fuae-luah oo-fu de-raah ahaen-de-ra/
