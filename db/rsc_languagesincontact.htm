SiteLanguages in Contact
DescriptionForms of language contact : contact languages, pidgins, creole, mixed languages, ... A mixed language is often a de facto conlang : Media Lengua-ga asi Ingichu-munda Castallanu-da abla-na kiri-xu-sha Media Lengua-TOP thus Quechua-from Spanish-ACC talk-NOM want-PROG-SUB "Media Lengua is like this, if you want to go from talking Quechua to Spanish, no abla-naku-ndu-mi asi chaupi-ga Castallanu laya, i chaupa-ga Ingichu laya abla-ri-na ga-n. not talk-PL-SUB-AFF thus half-TOP Spanish like and half-TOP Quechua like talk-REFL-NOM be-3 but you can't, then you talk half like Spanish and half like Quechua."
