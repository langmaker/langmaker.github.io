AuthorWill McGree
Script TypeAlphabet
DescriptionMassive revision: d10.m12.y04.h18.m14(GMT) Complete rehash of all the shapes to allow cursive writing and to improve on the aestethic quality. Vowel shapes redesigned, some new. E must be written separately to differentiate it from the connector lines. Otherwise, the recommended combination is consonant cluster-vowel, leaving a space afterwards. Might build a website about it later if I get enough interest generated.
InfluencesThe original English Shorthand charset.
