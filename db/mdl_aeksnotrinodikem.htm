Aeks Nótrï Nódikem (Aesthetic Kunstler Script No. 3 in No Discernable Key Minor) - Conlang Profile   Advanced
Language NameAeks Nótrï Nódikem
Language AuthorNiki Eve White
Year Began2001
SiteAeks Nótrï Nódikem
Broken LinkNo
Site LanguageEnglish
Site AuthorNiki Eve White
Language Typefictional language
UniquenessAeks is intended for the artist, designed to have ultimate flexibility in structure and vocabulary while maintaining logic through affixes that designate position in sentence structure.
Language SourcesNinyan (root language, possibly), German, Latin (I'm told), and Sindarin.
Design PrinciplesA system of affixes and roots makes the vocabulary limited literally to only the users imagination: ideal for creativity and artistry with five different scripts that can be used for embellishment of any work.
Interest Of OthersYes, there are a few outside participants who find the language entertaining and also use it as a means of secret communication, though that was not its original purpose.
QuotationIl Yae el il Thïelwint elninï en den Laknewinten Jineitornwï, il Thïelwint ekemmï aeterna en in mïpeil Waerfenwï Ẽt.
ExplanationA spirit is a (think→thought) (embody, passive tense = embodied) in the(acc.) [constrain→constraint][pl.]) [Imagine→Imagination, (/th/+/t/ = /th/ drops out) + possessive suffix (~nwï)], a thought (make, passive tense = made) eternal in a(acc) (hide, progressive tense) (society + possessive suffix ~enwï) script.
TranslationA spirit is a thought embodied in the constraints of imagination, a thought made eternal in a hiding society’s script.
