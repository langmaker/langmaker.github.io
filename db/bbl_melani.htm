Melani (I Sira Babelia) - Babel Text Profile   Advanced
SiteMelani
LanguageMelani
Language SubtitleI Sira Babelia
Year2002
TranslatorMichael Mechmann
IntroductionI translated for you a Babel Text for my conlang, Melani. This language has been my ongoing project for four years and is still evolving. Because of my constant revisions, the Babel Text will have to be updated constantly. I don't have much time to translate, so changes will be gradual, but eventually I hope to complete the grammar and all and finally stop having to update.
Verse 1I elsë visirumné nem' maconir cain i vesoroi drenoroi.
Verse 2Vor taner neselné rikanai, tainumelné u merocir nar i muros yè Shinar cai tan anemulné mar curer.
Verse 3Tainer apecné tainenor, norapec, "Menata, toi ecanarai poccoi cai sinacetan." Cai tainiram poccoi dai piccel cai biccen da sam.
Verse 4Cem tainer apecé, "Menata, toi etanacai da tanon u bicer, cai u sira, toinam sila mar i vel, cai toi nemaracai tanoner, rolsë toi ricesela nis lica i visa."
Verse 5I Makar cunané tairelë i bicer cai i sira, sagileroi etani.
Verse 6Ca' I Makar apecé, "Dila -- tainemela, cai tainam nem pecaunor; cai tigar ban cel i ceni-tainum pelloroi; ulsu ar tan luculë conela colanae."
Verse 7Menata, toi dacoata cai ribel tainum naconir, rilsa tan cunucan tainan."
Verse 8I Makar ricesé tainir lica nisae visiria, cai taner culaté etaner bici.
Verse 9Ri bicir nesala Babel, rila i Makar ribelé i nacon visiriae, cai icuri Tiner ricesé tainir nisae visiriae.
Notes 1A bit of pronunciation help: All c's are HARD: /k/ All ai's are pronounced: /eye/ All ae's are pronounced: /ay/ All i's are pronounced: /ee/ All a's are pronounced: /ah/ All é's are pronounced: /ey/ (as in hey) All ë's are written like that so you remember to pronounce them!
