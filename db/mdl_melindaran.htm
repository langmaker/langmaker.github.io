Melindaran - Conlang Profile   Advanced
Language NameMelindaran
Language AuthorRandal L. Lanning
Year Began2004
SiteMelindaran
Broken LinkNo
Site LanguageEnglish|Esperanto
Site AuthorRandal L. Lanning
Language Typepersonal language
UniquenessMost of it is a priori so far. There are no special letters and the grammar is a balance of simple, regular, agglutinative, and irregular. I'm trying to make it easy to speak and still sound natural. OK, it's probably not unique.
Language SourcesGerman, Klingon, Esperanto, Latin, Greek, English slang, a lot of apriori, and several inside jokes to myself.
Design PrinciplesSVO order primarily, although it doesn't have to be. Active verbs end in -de. Passive take the root. Nouns are always the root. Plurals are added with -ams unless they follow a number in which case they do not change. Adjectives end in -al. Adverbs in -era.
Interest Of OthersMelinda R. - I named the language after her and she inspired me to write it. It's a personal language that I doubt anyone else would want to speak but one never knows. If you do, have fun. :)
