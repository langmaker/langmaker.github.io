AuthorMattias Persson
Script TypeAlphabet
DescriptionPatoukii is a true alphabet with a rather unusual distribution of its sounds - 12 consonants and 33 vowels (because of tones).
InfluencesPatoukii is inspired by one of Herman Miller's scripts (I do not remember its name right now, but that script also has distinct letters for each vowel-tone combination).
