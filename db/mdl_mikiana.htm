Mikiana - Conlang Profile   Advanced
Language NameMikiana
Language AuthorJonathan Bettencourt
Year Began2002
SiteMikiana
Broken LinkNo
Site LanguageEnglish
Site AuthorJonathan Bettencourt
Language Typefictional language
UniquenessMikiana is the native language of Mika City. It was spoken by Mikianans many years ago, and today by Shawna Hart and her sister Terri. The grammar rules are similar to English. It is easy to learn because there are no irregular verbs or conjugations or genders or subject-verb agreement or stem changes or other stuff like that.
Language SourcesEnglish, other Indo-European languages, names of people I know.
Design PrinciplesBasically to try making the language as easy as possible, and to immortalize some people I know.
Interest Of OthersAnybody who has become interested in Mika City cartoons and has heard of the language. Mostly this would be my closest friends and the few people who stumble across my web site.
Conculture Noun PhraseMikiana people,
QuotationNa Mi~kana Cithra erra sha mes personem tith gardella diyana tath thea sabren evra denna. -- Xenymmenen
ExplanationIn Mika City there are many people with blonde hair that they brush-like-you-do-with-a-little-girl's-hair every day. -- Not-Named
TranslationIn Mika City there are many people with blonde hair that they brush every day. -- Anonymous
