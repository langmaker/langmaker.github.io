Ksokish - Babel Text Profile   Advanced
LanguageKsokish
Year2002
IntroductionJust some notes on pronunciation:
'kh' is pronounced like the German 'ch'
'aa', 'oo', 'uu' are normally just the letter with umlauts
'aa' -like 'a' in father
'oo' - like 'o' in ocean, but rounded much more
'uu' - like the German 'uu' or a rounded 'u'
Verse 1Faskhup waji on dam marok juirama.
Verse 2Wal nogo suumo jol hi, cim fasdfu fos Sonar ler, wal cim dapo bwbanista.
Verse 3Waino sefito ye khanifas, Jik yatil dhabyibel, wal sim jik cim jabyisal. Cim yatil telu wu dhabyibel, wal cim calik yacilik juirama.
Verse 4Cim khanifas, Jik tsats wal farino fabyisdhubel, mir daso henza ye cepim. Waal jik ei fyimcu, tim jik feskhup weji nim kerbwbabyiso yak.
Verse 5Okiksook tsats wal farino cu doki ksook funisdhubel yak etnuig sayud.
Verse 6Okiksook naar, Nosayud! Ksooki dam yekaar, wal cim on dam marok jyaarama. Wal diso kyaakakim; wal tudiro, cim etso cu cim fasdhanibel nu et hyaakakim yak.
Verse 7Jek faskhup nuug waal marok cim asoi, waal cim safito et itamabyada.
Verse 8Okiksook cim dapo jol faskhup nim kairbwbasto waal tsauts et faisdhubel yak.
Verse 9Dam kumekmuleti foomcu Babel; Okiksook marok fooskhup asoi; Waal Okiksook cim dapo jol faskhup nim kairbwbasto.
