UniquenessAnklis has changed dramatically over the past year that I have been working on it. Anklis has been heavily inspired by some of the ideas of Basic Anglo-Saxon English and features only words derived from Germanic languages. More on this will be explained when I post a language history. It is still based on Modern American English, but with various sound changes and shifts, as well as a phonetic alphabet and simplification of grammar rules. Website will be up soon.
Language SourcesMainly Modern American English but due to recent changes in the ideology of Anklis, lots of German vocabulary and structure has been implemented (though not exactly as is done in German and other Germanic languages).
