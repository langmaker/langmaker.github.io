Wordpetronoia
Part of Speechn.
Etymology[< petro(leum) + (para)noia.]
Coined ByOther (specified in Etymology)
DefinitionAn excessive fear or worry over the future price of oil. "The latest OPEC meeting was preceded by a rash of petronoia, causing gas prices to jump sharply."
