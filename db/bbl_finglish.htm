TranslatorOmid Ghayour
Verse 1va tamame zamin yek zaban bud va kalamate kami
Verse 2va an zamani bud ke mohajerat kardand az ghedem/shargh, [anan] dashti dar sarzamine shin'ar yaftand va anja ra shokhm zadand.
Verse 3va [yek] nafar be dustash goft: "bia! khesht ha ra kesht migirim va atash miafruzim." va khesht barayeshan sang va gel barateshan mavad bud.
Verse 4va anha goftand: "bia! baraye khod shahri misazim va borji va sar an be falak, va baraye kod nami khahim yaft, va bar ruye tamame zamin parakande khahim shod.
Verse 5va YHVH nazel shod ta nazer bashad bar shahr va borgi ke farzandane ensan sakhtand.
Verse 6va YHVH farmud: "han, melate vahed, va [anga] yek zaban baraye hame anha hast, va in chizi ast ke anha bayad shoru konand. va hala, an az anha darigh dashte nemishavad, har anche anha [bekhahand] tarh bekeshand ta anjam dahand.
Verse 7bia! ma nazel khahim shod va anga zabanhayeshan ra dar ham pichide khahim kard, ke [anha] [har] kasi zaban dustash ra nakhahad fahmid.
Verse 8va YHVH az anga anha ra bar ruye tamame zamin parakande kard, va anha sakhtan shahr ra motevagef kardand.
Verse 9banabarin namash ra "babel" nahadand, zira anga YHVH zabane tamame zamin ra darham pichid, va az anga YHVH anha ra bar ruye tamame zamin parakande kard.
