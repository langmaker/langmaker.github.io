UniquenessA trigger language with many nasals, liquids, and /a/'s.
Language SourcesAustronesian.
Design PrinciplesThe language is thought to be quite simplistic, without severe irregularities. Its morphology is agglutinating, which means loads of pre- and suffixes.
Interest Of OthersReaders of the Zompist Bulletin Board.
QuotationEri silveváng aibannama padangin. Nivaie evaenain eri ming silvoieváng caparei.

TranslationOn ne voit bien qu'avec le coeur. L'essentiel est invisible pour les yeux." -- One can only see well with one's heart. The essential is invisible for the eyes.
