IntroductionVozgian is a North-Slavonic conlang, supposedly spoken in the Vozgian Autonomous Republic, somewhere in the North of Russia. I started creating it back in 1996 an, as might be expected, work is still in progress.

In 2004, the language was restarted from scratch. This Babel Text translation represents the old version of the language and therefore may be considered obsolete.
