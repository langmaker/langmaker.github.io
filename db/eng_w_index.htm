wackademia - 1. The study of strange phenomena such as the Bermuda Triangle, crop circles, UFOs or the Loch Ness Monster. 2. A subgroup of academics who, by virtue of their eccentric views, develop strange social theories (e.g., democratic parenting, marriage as legalized rape) that they then project on their unsuspecting pupils.

weinworthy - Refers to a piece of fiction or episode of a TV show that is so imaginative or creative, that it could have been created by brilliant author Stanley G. Weinbaum. (e.g. "Last night's episode of Voyager was almost weinworthy!").
