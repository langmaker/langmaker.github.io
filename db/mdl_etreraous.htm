Etrer'aous - Conlang Profile   Advanced
Language NameEtrer'aous
Language AuthorDrew Fischer
Year Began2001
SiteEtrer'aous
Broken LinkNo
Site LanguageEnglish
Language Typepersonal language
UniquenessThis language has it's own sound ... almost a mix between middle-eastern, elvish dialects, and some of almost every language all combined in one to make it sound like its own and origonal. This is different also because the teachers of this language are greatly involved and concerned with the education of the learners.
Language SourcesSome other languages that inspired it were: Elvish (and other Tolkien languages) German GRAMMAR Plus sounds of middle-east, french, and other european languages.
Interest Of OthersThere are several others who are signed up (for FREE, of course) that are active in learning and teaching. They are around-the-clock helpers and active learners. They wish to be a part of the feeling you take away when you learn something unique, mysterious, and little known and spread it to others for fun. It's a fun, yet serious, language at the same time, and that's what makes it attractive to most people!
