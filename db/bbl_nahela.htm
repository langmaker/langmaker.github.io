Nahéla - Babel Text Profile   Advanced
LanguageNahéla
Year2000
TranslatorGustaf Hansson
IntroductionMi leva onze Moseme Mi appe onzoze
Verse 1Nis mi selime on onzeliesle ia on zápiesel.
Verse 2Sinalihe welil mifolas nam peljalazle heliz ia lemen zigyvilici.
Verse 3Onelaqil hylyci: Osav zi, qailal zi lánîzó ia zén cenna iasiqiv zi. Halilenie lánîzó nemeci, ia zîminnan ommananlenie.
Verse 4Sint hylyci: Osav zi, mia lenenn imonav zi, yhâlelenmae fâ mi cinsa heses, fáne halalellev qâsalizy ia fáne mi zéneelisize loz ilnozavell zi.
Verse 5Epa ine zalal mi lenenn ia mi yhâlelen, fâ mi pêe imonâ, azasiiselil Si.
Verse 6Hylysi Si: Cilon on pêece, fâ mi lopeciesle eselezap, sâ alalezeci qylyv, qi cannafelin òalanzesa nacy loqyv, fâ sinqylyc qylyv.
Verse 7Ine ammellev canname esle, fâ loz onelaqil halac, azaviselil zi.
Verse 8Pònie mi zéneelisize cenna ilnozasi Si, ia imonan mi lenenn fenneci.
Verse 9Pelin Papel o anvalelli òylizesi sâ, qi lemen esle mi zénieli ammellesi Si. Hemenfo mi zénielisize cenna ilnozasi Si.
Notes 1(elements in bold are loanwords from Cânnânn Naewinn. Their C.N. counterparts are shown in their modern forms) 1. Nis mi seli-me on on-ze-l-i-es-le ia on záp-i-es-el. nîs, seîl, mê 2. Sinal-ihe wel-il mi-fol-as nam pelj-a-l-az-le heli-z ia lemen ziq-y-v-ili-ci. fá, aus, trig- 3. On-elaq-il hyly-ci: Os-a-v zi, qail-a-l zi lánîzó ia zén cenna ilasiq-i-v zi. îr, gâwr-, lãcity, bylasig- Hali-len-ie lánîzó neme-ci, ia zîminnan omm-an-an-len-ie. diminnin, wmm 4. Sint hyly-ci: Osa-v zi, mia len-enn imon-a-v zi, yh-âl-el-en-mae fâ mi cinsa hese-s, fáne hal-al-ell-e-v qâs-a-l-iz-y ia fáne mi zén-e-eli-size loz ilnoz-a-v-ell zi. zinn, fynn, mê, cinnsagh, gâlh-, sî 5. Epa ine zal-a-l mi len-enn ia mi yh-âl-el-en, fâ mi pê-e imon-â, az-a-si-isel-il Si. âp, athr- 6.
