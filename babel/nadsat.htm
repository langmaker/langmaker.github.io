1N. And all the earth had one voice and one tongue.



2Na. As men moved eastward, they found a plain in Shinar and they

2Nb. made it their house place.



3Na. And they said one to another, "Go to, let us make bricks, and

3Nb. burn them good." And they had brick for stone, and slime they

3Nc. had for mortar.



4Na. And they said, "Go to, let us build us a city, and a tower,

4Nb. whose top will go up as high as god and all his angels; and let us

4Nc. make a big name for ourselves and not be scattered over the face

4Nd. of the whole earth."



5Na. And the Lord God came down to see the city and the tower, which the

5Nb. children of men builded.



6Na. And the Lord said, "See, they are all one people and have all

6Nb. one tongue; and this they begin to do: and now nothing will

6Nc. be kept from them, which they have imagined to do.



7Na. Go to, let us go down and make their tongue noise, so they will

7Nb. not understand one another."



8Na. So the Lord God sent them abroad upon the face of the earth; and

8Nb. they left off building the city.



9Na. So the name of it is Babel, because there the Lord God and all

9Nb. his angels took away the meaning of all tongues; and from there

9Nc. the Lord sent them away over all the face of the earth.
