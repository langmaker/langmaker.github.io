Dha-Patu

1. O, dha mund akhsha ta ghoti tiuth dha patu isti, dhe zier d'nar.

2. O, uo to dhoar, to oliebong, ta zmoiber dha-zhdeggharb Shinar, ta viot dha-su.

3. O, dhe shob'd to patu orus: "E! Ua to telovume vruithrad." Dhi vumetelo tu ghoti uo; dhi argil to khneu dhi motu.

4. O, uo ta patu "E! Ua ta mian dha azim, dha tur ensi; dha-turagat to khneu ones dha-iral; ua to atnimba, tu oliezek mrag, re dha-mundupan."

5. O, Demiurgos to ebongstui ta-prash dha-azim, dha-tur ensi, re tu mian dhe idao dhi-Shob'd.

6. O, Demiurgos ta patu "Dha thome isti ta ghoti akhsha dha patu isti; re tu srot evne uo. Aire, uo tu englo mrag, re tu iras to-srot uo.

7. "E! Ua to ebongstui aire, ta psat dha-patu uo; uo to afiv orus mrag dha-patu."

8. O, Demiurgos ta oliezek uo, re dha-mundupan akhsha; uo to oghub ta-mian dha-azim.

9. Dha-siol to atni "Babel," re tu ghosr, Demiurgos ta psat ones dha-patu dha-mund akhsha, ta oliezek uo, re dha-mundupan.

Pronunciation Notes

  • Vowels are as in (e.g.) Spanish; pronounced as diphthongs when juxtaposed, even between words.
  • The apostrophe represents a weak vowel, realized in most dialects as schwa, elided in liaison.
  • Digraphs th, dh as 'thin, then' respectively; gh, kh as Modern Greek ghama, khi.
  • G is always hard, as in 'gig'.
