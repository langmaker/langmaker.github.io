Model Language Resource Links

One of the best sites describing how to build your own languages is Mark Rosenfelder's The Language Construction Kit.

Another good introductory kit is David Dynes' How To Invent A Real Language (H2IARL).

For good ideas on how to summarize your language's grammar, check out this Grammar Sketch Outline, originally intended for natural languages.

If you need more information about the interrelationships of different species of plants and animals, check out The Tree of Life.
