Correlatives
Correlatives are the words used to phrase questions that require specific answers (rather than yes/no) and their corresponding general answers.  The "who, what, when, where and why" questions, and the replies of "here, there, everywhere, nowhere, somewhere".

Correlatives are not a part of speech, but a set of nouns, noun phrases and adverbs.
