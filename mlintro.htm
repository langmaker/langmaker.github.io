Introduction

From Afrihili to Zireenka, this index lists all the model languages that I am familiar with that have a presence on the Internet. I have included links to all the languages I have come across, but have only written descriptions of those languages that I am familiar with.

Please e-mail me and tell me about other languages I should include and notify me if you come across any broken links.

The links were originally derived from the home pages of the following individuals:

  • Chris Bogart
  • Dirk Elzinga
  • Rick Harrison
  • Donald Harlow
  • Richard Kennaway

Check out their pages for different perspectives on these languages and for languages that I missed.
